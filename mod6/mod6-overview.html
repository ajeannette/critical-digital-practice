This module will explore data, what it is, who uses it, and the societal implications surrounding it. Hand-in-hand with the topic of data is the issue of privacy and what we give up when choosing convenience over informed skepticism when interacting with online services.

Learning outcomes

After going through this module, you will:

1. Identify data in various academic and digital contexts.
2. Understand how and what data is collected online.
3. Define metadata as well as why and how different standards are developed.
4. Think critically about the implications of our online interactions.
